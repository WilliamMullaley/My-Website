William Mullaley Story

William Mullaley is an emerging artist currently studying at Wesley College. As a proud Widi Yamitji Noongar man, he has a deep personal connection to his Indigenous culture, which profoundly influences his work. In addition to his academic pursuits, he serves as a youth leader at MADALAH, an organization supporting Indigenous students through education. William's artworks and designs, inspired by his heritage, have garnered numerous awards, highlighting his talent and the rich cultural narratives he brings to the art world.
